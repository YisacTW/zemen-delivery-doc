Welcome to BizFlows' documentation!
===================================
*BizFlows* is an innovative business management platform tailored
specifically for **small and medium-sized enterprises**. Leveraging 
cutting-edge technology, BizFlows streamlines various business processes,
providing users with real-time delivery tracking, automated invoicing,
AI-powered inventory forecasting, and customer segmentation tools. The 
platform covers a wide range of features relevant to modern businesses,
ensuring comprehensive management and efficiency for users in the DMV area.
