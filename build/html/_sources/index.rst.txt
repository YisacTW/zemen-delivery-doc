Welcome to TechTalk AI's documentation!
=======================================
*TechTalk AI* is an innovative interview preparation app tailored
specifically for **Software Engineering** and **Product Management**
candidates. Leveraging cutting-edge AI technology, TechTalk AI simulates 
real-time interview scenarios, providing users with practice questions,
instant feedback, and detailed analytics to help them ace their
interviews. The app covers a wide range of topics relevant to these
fields, ensuring comprehensive preparation and confidence for users.
